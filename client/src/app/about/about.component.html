What is this?

Nouveau Vin is an Angular application for browsing professional wine reviews. The back end is comprised of an Express/Node server and MariaDB (MySQL).

This project was inspired by a school group project created in Spring 2019. The old project was also an application (AngularJS) for browsing wine reviews. It also used an earlier version of Express/Node, and MariaDB. It provided some basic querying functionality, with unoptimized sorting and searching, as well as a skeleton for browsing by a single feature. Despite the visions for more functionality, nothing further was developed by our team after the spring term.

Check out the Github page here.

The Data

The data contains over 100,000 reviews from professional wine critics and was acquired from Kaggle. The data set and my representation of it are available under the Creative Commons license BY-NC-SA 4.0.

The Reviews

Each review card is headlined with the rating of the wine (out of 100 points), the winery, vintage, and designation. Below this is province/country of the winery and the estimated price per bottle.

The critic's description of the wine is in the middle. Below that is the name of the critic and their Twitter handle (if any). Clicking the buttons will navigate you to reviews by that critic and to their Twitter feed, respectively.

The reviews can vary considerably in content. In addition to Twitter handles, some reviews are without an estimated price or province, while others vary in the format of the title altogether. However, most of the reviews follow the outlined format.
